About 101Headshots

Founded in 2015 by master photographer Bryan Engel, 101Headshots emerged from a vision to provide Lee's Summit professionals with magazine-quality headshots at accessible prices. What started as a small studio in downtown Lee's Summit has grown into the area's most trusted corporate photography service, having captured over 5,000 professionals across various industries.

Our mission is to help every client present their most confident and authentic professional image. We believe in the power of first impressions and are committed to delivering exceptional quality, personalized service, and a comfortable photography experience. Our core values of excellence, authenticity, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do.
